Who Made Stranger Things?

Stranger Things was created and directed by Matt and Ross Duffer, widely known as the Duffer Brothers. They are twins who grew up immersed in 1980s pop culture, including movies by Steven Spielberg and horror novels by Stephen King. Inspired by these influences, they wrote a story about a small town filled with mystery, supernatural powers, and childhood friendships.
