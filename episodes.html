Seasons

Season 1

Set in the 1980s, a young boy disappears in Hawkins, Indiana. His friends discover a girl with supernatural powers and uncover the existence of a secretive government lab experimenting on a parallel dimension called the Upside Down.

Season 2

A year after Will's rescue, the town faces a new threat from the Upside Down. Eleven discovers her past and abilities while friends band together to stop a deadly creature known as the Mind Flayer from taking over Hawkins.

Season 3

The summer of 1985 brings new enemies and a mall. Strange events continue as the Mind Flayer returns stronger, targeting the group. New relationships and betrayals develop as the town fights for survival.

Season 4

Several months after the events of Season 3, dark forces gather power outside Hawkins. Eleven struggles with losing her powers while friends uncover shocking secrets, leading to a confrontation with the terrifying Vecna.

Season 5

The final season sees the heroes and villains collide in an epic battle for Hawkins and beyond. Mysteries from past seasons come to light, old enemies return, and the story reaches a climactic, emotional conclusion.
